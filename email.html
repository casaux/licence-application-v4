Dear Mark Woden,

You have completed the theory and practical requirements (including a successful flight test) required for a flight crew licence.

In order to be granted and sent your licence, you must login to myCASA and complete the following steps:

  1. Review and confirm flight crew licence application details
  2. Verify your aviation security identification card (ASIC)
  3. Consent to CASA accessing your ASIC photograph
  4. Complete a fit and proper person declaration
  5. Pay for your licence application

If you cannot complete all of these steps online, you will need to submit a form to complete your application. More information on this process can be found on the CASA website.
