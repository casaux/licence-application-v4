ASIC card verification

The Aviation Transport Security Regulations does not permit CASA to issue a licence unless the applicant has undergone a security check. Holding an Aviation security identification card (ASIC) satisfies this requirement.

If you do not yet have an ASIC, or you have an AVID, you cannot finish this application online and will need to apply for your licence using the relevant form.

Your issuing body is who you applied to and received your ASIC from. It may be printed on the front or back of your ASIC card. If you're unsure of this, please refer to any correspondence you received regarding your ASIC application or contact your flight school.
Please enter the number shown on your ASIC. This is usually at the bottom of the ASIC and may contain letters and dashes. Please enter all the characters as shown on your ASIC.
Your CASA ASIC number is your ARN
Your ASIC issue number appears after your ASIC number a dash.
Please enter your first name as shown on card
