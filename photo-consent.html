Lodgement of photograph

CASA requires your photograph under regulation 61.155 of the Civil Aviation Safety Regulations 1998 (CASR) to issue a flight crew licence to you. As an aviation security identification card (ASIC) holder, CASA is able to use the photograph you supplied for your ASIC for this purpose.

By giving your consent below CASA will receive, store and use your ASIC photo in the ways explained below:

  • it will be stored on CASA’s licensing portal for access by authorised CASA officers
  • it will be stored on CASA’s Medical Records System for access by authorised CASA officers and your Designated Aviation Medical Examiner (DAME). This will also enable the DAME to verify under CASR 67.170 that you are who you claim to be when being medically examined
  • it may be displayed on any flight crew licence issued to you
  • it may otherwise be used or disclosed in accordance with CASA’s privacy statement

Consent to use photograph

I consent to the Department of Home Affairs’ Auscheck database disclosing to CASA (a) confirmation of the validity or invalidity of my ASIC, and (b) the photo that I provided as part of my ASIC application. I also consent to the photo being used for the above purposes.
