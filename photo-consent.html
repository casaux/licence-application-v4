Lodgement of photograph

CASA requires your photograph under Civil Aviation Safety Regulations 1998 (CASR) 61.155 to issue a flight crew licence to you. As a valid aviation security identification card (ASIC) holder, CASA is able to use the photograph supplied for your ASIC for this purpose.

By giving your consent below CASA will receive and store your ASIC photo in the ways explained below.

  • It will be stored on CASA’s licensing portal for access by authorised CASA officers
  • It will be stored on CASA’s Medical Records System for access by authorised CASA officers and your Designated Aviation Medical Examiner (DAME). This will also enable a DAME to verify under CASR 67.170 that you are who you claim to be when being medically examined
  • displayed on your flight crew or air traffic control licence
  • otherwise disclosed in accordance with CASA’s privacy statement

If you do not consent to CASA using this photograph, you cannot finish this application online and will need to apply for your licence using the relevant form.

Consent to use photograph

I give my consent for Auscheck or any ASIC issuing body to disclose a copy of any photograph of myself that they hold to CASA for the above purposes.
